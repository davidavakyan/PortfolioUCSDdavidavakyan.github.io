About Me!

I am a second year undergraduate at UCSD who is absolutely enthralled by the possibility of one day practicing medicine. Most of my headspace as a student has been characerized by the process of bolstering myself to become a great physician one day. As a Neurobiology major, I rally myself towards my accomplishments with this greater goal in mind; I fulfill executive roles in student organizations, adamantly chase past-times that either are (tennis; mentorship) or may become passions of mine (programming, research). I’ve become rather obsessed with learning as much as possible in this relatively short period of time so that I can identify as someone that I feel is well equipped to serve patients in clinical spaces. Although I’m still short of two decades on this planet, I want to enter such spaces, working alongside those with years of experience practicing medicine, conducting investigations in the lab, and in a meaningful way, exploring the frontiers that are adressed by the broad umbrella of neurosciences.
